Our Story

Nine Years Ago

Brandon and I met way back in high school through my maid of honour Sara. I was practicing the clarinet before band practice and truly had no interest in taking time out of my practicing to meet two random guys she brought to me. Over the next two years we became very good friends and we started dating after graduating. I was both nervous and excited to start dating my best male friend. When asked, Brandon sums up his feelings about this move as “I was happy”. Over the next few years, we moved to kingston for post-secondary school and we lived in a few different apartments together. In Kingston we took our relationship to the next level and adopted our fur baby Basie together. Brandon then moved to Toronto for work and I reluctantly went to the big city eight months later. We shared our love of music together by attending many shows and concerts. When offered a position in my field of work, I moved closer to home and Brandon joined me a few months later. We are now living in Frankford until we purchase our first home together.
